✨ What is Expieder?

Expieder is an Augmented Reality platform that enables spatial social interactions, starting with your smartphone. Pin and discover digital content like messages, media, and profiles; all tied to real-world locations. Whether it's outside your favorite café or along a mountain trail, you leave a part of you behind, and someone else discovers it.

🔍 Discover Social Layers Around You

  • Explore Instagram posts, reels, and social profiles embedded into your environment.
  • Walk through a park and stumble across someone's art.
  • Look up and find a pinned Spotify playlist on a rooftop.

Social media becomes spatial, woven into the real world.

🚀 How It Works

  • 📍 Spatial Pinning: Drop messages, media, memories at real-world locations.
  • 🧭 Explore with AR: Use your camera to view your surroundings and see content layered in real-time.
  • 🔗 Social Layer: Find Instagram, LinkedIn & other social posts, Spotify playlists, public profiles immersed nearby.
